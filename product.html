AOD9604

Anti-Obesity Drug 9604 is a modified form of amino acids 176-191 of the GH polypeptide.

Category: Peptides

AOD9604 is a modified form of amino acids 176-191 of the GH polypeptide. Investigators at Monash University discovered that the fat-reducing effects of GH appear to be controlled by a small region near one end of the GH molecule. This region, which consists of amino acids 176-191, is less than 10% of the total size of the GH molecule and appears to have no effect on growth or insulin resistance.
